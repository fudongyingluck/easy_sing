# Privacy Policy for Easy Sing (轻松练唱)

**Last updated: April 7, 2026**

## Overview

Easy Sing ("the App") is developed and operated by fudongyingluck@163.com ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. **We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
- We do not collect location data.
- We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
- We do not share any data with third parties.
- We do not use advertising networks.

---

## Microphone Access

The App requests access to your device's microphone **solely** to detect pitch in real time for vocal practice. All audio processing is performed entirely on your device.

- No audio recordings are uploaded to any server.
- No audio data leaves your device.
- Recordings are saved locally on your device only and can be deleted by you at any time.

---

## Data Storage

Any data generated by the App (such as practice recordings) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not accessible to us and is never transmitted off your device.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
